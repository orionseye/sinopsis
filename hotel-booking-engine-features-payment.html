Secured
Octabytes CRS handles payment gateways according to the highest security standards. There is no use of any "ready packed" solutions, nor 3rd party applications. There is no compromise when it comes to protect customer sensitive data.

PAYMENT/GUARANTEE MODES

Octabytes CRS has payment tool that can automatically charge credit cards, and transfer funds, directly to hotel account. The system supports multiple payment gateways and multiple currencies, several processors can be attached at the same time. Credit cards info is handled with maximum care and security. For security reasons the clients personal info is encrypted and kept on the secure server.

CREDIT CARDS PROCESSING

The highest level of credit cards processing is applied, using secure server with 128 bit encryption. The system issues payment forms or reservation confirmation/voucher valid for boarder crossing purposes where applicable.

  • Payment with credit any cards (AMEX, VISA, MasterCard etc.)
  • Credit card processing (charge/verify) in real-time
  • System can be linked to any card processor for credit or debit cards

CLIENTS WITHOUT CREDIT CARDS

Clients without credit cards can book via Octabytes CRS as well. They will be offered to pay guarantee down payment instead of guarantee by credit card. Down payments are governed by the sales policies. Each rate plan can have its own sales policy.


SECURE TRANSACTIONS

Octabytes CRS booking engine is compliant with PCI DSS security standard in credit card processing. PCI certification provides the highest security level for online transactions available today. Authorization and survey of the network security is done by banks and credit card companies (VISA/ MasterCard/AMEX), before going live into the market. Rest assured that your, and your customers data, is protected against elevated risk of credit card fraud.
