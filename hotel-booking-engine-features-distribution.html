GDS INTERFACE

Seamless two-way interface to Sabre, Amadeus, Worldspan and Galileo reservation systems. Octabytes CRS users can easily offer their inventory to huge community of travel agents linked to GDS systems, or hundreds of Internet booking sites via Pegasus ODD. The reservations from these channels are treated in the same way as reservations originating from hotels own web site, including full statistics and reporting.

Real time adjustments to prices, and room availability via booking engine and across all 4 GDS. Reservations via GDS can be viewed, cancelled or modified via online wherever you are.

Free placement on the Pegasus ODD powered travel sites: over 1200 major and minor sites such as Expedia, Travelocity, Orbitz, Hotels.com, and many more..


RESERVATIONS FLOW

Reservations are flowing from various sales channels:

Property web site & contracted distribution

  • Individual guests
  • Groups
  • Partners/Agents
  • Call centres
  • GDS (Global distribution systems)
  • IDS (Internet distribution systems)
  • ADS (Alternative distribution systems)
  • Third party web sites

You can set your distribution channels in any way, either each property in a group separated, or chain related distribution. Octabytes CRS supports any major chain code you are member of, on any distribution channels, at unlimited number of properties per chain. The engine supports Pegasus (UZ), Trust International (YH,LX,LH), Reconline (ON), Supranational (SX), HotelREZ (HO) or any other chain code.
